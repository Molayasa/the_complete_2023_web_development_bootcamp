The Best Movies According to Paulina

This is a beautiful anime movie, with a lovely and cuddly creature known as Totoro.

Howl no Ugoku Shiro

Also known as Howl's Moving Castle, is a great movie with magic characters and cute scenes.

Mary to Majo no Hana
